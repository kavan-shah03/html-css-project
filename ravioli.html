Ravioli

Ravioli, an iconic Italian dish, is a testament to the culinary heritage of Italy. These delectable pockets of pasta encapsulate a rich history of craftsmanship and flavor. With its origins deeply rooted in the medieval times of Lombardy and Liguria, ravioli has evolved into a timeless classic cherished for its versatility and delicate taste. Traditionally stuffed with a blend of ricotta, spinach, and herbs, and served with an array of tantalizing sauces, ravioli continues to captivate food enthusiasts worldwide with its exquisite taste and texture.

Recipe for Classic Ricotta and Spinach Ravioli:

Ingredients

  1. For the Pasta Dough:
    • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
    • 3 large eggs
    • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
    • A pinch of salt
  2. For the Filling:
    • 1 cup of fresh ricotta cheese
    • 1 cup of cooked spinach, drained and finely chopped
    • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
    • 1/4 teaspoon of freshly grated nutmeg
    • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  3. For the Sauce:
    • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
    • Fresh sage leaves
    • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish
    • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

01

Prepare the Pasta Dough:

  • On a clean surface, form a mound with the flour and make a well in the center.
  • Crack the eggs into the well, add olive oil and a pinch of salt.
  • Gradually incorporate the flour into the eggs until a dough forms.
  • Knead the dough for about 8-10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  • Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and let it rest for 30 minutes at room temperature.

02

Make the Filling:

  • In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta, cooked spinach, grated Parmesan, nutmeg, salt, and pepper.
  • Mix the ingredients thoroughly until a smooth, well-combined mixture is achieved.

03

Roll out the Pasta Dough:

  • Divide the rested dough into smaller portions.
  • Roll out each portion of the dough using a pasta machine or a rolling pin until it is thin and translucent.

04

Assemble the Ravioli:

  • Place small dollops of the filling evenly spaced on one sheet of the rolled-out dough.
  • Gently lay another sheet of rolled-out dough on top.
  • Press the sheets together around the mounds of filling, ensuring there are no air pockets.

05

Cut and Cook the Ravioli:

  • Use a ravioli cutter or a knife to cut the ravioli into desired shapes.
  • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and carefully add the ravioli.
  • Cook for about 3-4 minutes or until they float to the surface.
  • Remove the ravioli from the water using a slotted spoon and set them aside.

06

Prepare the Sauce and Serve:

  • In a separate pan, melt the butter over medium heat and add the fresh sage leaves.
  • Cook until the butter turns a light golden brown and the sage becomes crisp.
  • Drizzle the sage-infused butter over the cooked ravioli and garnish with grated Parmesan, salt, and pepper to taste.
  • Serve immediately, allowing the flavors to entice the palate with their delightful harmony.

Experience the richness of Italy with our delectable Ravioli, featuring layers of savory flavors that transport you to the heart of Italian culinary traditions. Buon appetito!
