Lasagna

Lasagna is a popular Italian dish that consists of multiple layers of wide, flat pasta, typically with alternating layers of meat, vegetables, cheese, and various seasonings. The pasta is often accompanied by a rich, thick sauce, such as a bolognese or marinara sauce, which binds the layers together. Cheese, usually ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan, is spread between the layers and often forms a deliciously gooey and golden crust on top when baked. Lasagna is known for its comforting, hearty, and flavorful qualities, making it a favorite dish for many around the world.

Recipe for Traditional Lasagna

Ingredients

  1. For the Meat Ragù:
    • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
    • 1/2 pound (225g) ground pork
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 28 ounces (800g) canned crushed tomatoes
    • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
    • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Fresh basil and oregano for seasoning
  2. For the Béchamel Sauce:
    • 4 cups (1 liter) whole milk
    • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter
    • 1/2 cup (60g) all-purpose flour
    • Salt, pepper, and nutmeg to taste
  3. For Assembly:
    • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked al dente
    • 2 cups (200g) shredded mozzarella cheese
    • 1 cup (100g) grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

01

Preparing the Meat Ragù:

  • In a large saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  • Add chopped onion and garlic and sauté until translucent.
  • Add the ground beef and pork, breaking it up into small pieces, and cook until browned.
  • Pour in the red wine (if using) and let it simmer for a few minutes.
  • Stir in the crushed tomatoes and tomato paste.
  • Season with salt, pepper, basil, and oregano.
  • Simmer the ragù on low heat for at least 1 hour, stirring occasionally.

02

Preparing the Béchamel Sauce:

  • In a separate saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  • Add the flour and whisk continuously to create a smooth roux.
  • Gradually add the milk while continuing to whisk, ensuring no lumps form.
  • Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg.
  • Simmer the béchamel until it thickens, stirring constantly.

03

Assembly:

  • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • In a large baking dish, spread a thin layer of béchamel sauce on the bottom.
  • Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the béchamel.
  • Add a layer of meat ragù, followed by a layer of béchamel sauce and a sprinkling of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  • Repeat the process, creating multiple layers until the ingredients are used up, finishing with a layer of cheese on top.

04

Baking:

  • Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes.
  • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the top is golden and bubbling.

05

Serving:

  • Let the lasagna rest for a few minutes before serving to allow the layers to set.
  • Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves and an extra sprinkle of Parmesan cheese.

Enjoy this Italian masterpiece – the rich, indulgent layers of Lasagna will delight your taste buds and transport you to the heart of Italy. Buon appetito!
